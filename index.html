Why Yattavision?

We bring experiences from the screen right into your life!

15 MP Camera

Share your day-to-day stories. Capture all of the can’t miss moments with Yattavision’s built-in 15 megapixel camera. Once you capture your moment in time, instantly upload your photos and videos to the social media site of your choice!

Smart-Scan

On the fence about which new television to buy? Need more product info on an item at the grocery store? Use Yattavision’s Smart-Scan vision to bring up a list of search results and get all of the details you need in the blink of an eye!

World Display

World-Display shows you the information you want on command, with a transparent overlay that won't distract you from the road ahead. Just swipe your fingers in front of your face to navigate through apps or dismiss notifications!

Yattassistant

Need some information? Just tap the bridge that connects your eyeglass lenses. Commuting updates, weather changes and daily calendar reminders are just some of the things covered by Yattasistant, the best voice recognition assistant on the market!
